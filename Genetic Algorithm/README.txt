Genetic Algorithm - Collateral Damage

Purpose:
To find the most optimal location to detonate a bomb, one that maximises the elimination of "bad" robots,
and minimises the elimination of "good" robots.

Controls:
[LEFT MOUSE BUTTON]: Execute the genetic algorithm to find the most optimal location to detonate a bomb.
[RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON]: Shuffle the positions of the robots.
[MOUSE SCROLL WHEEL]: Increase/decrease the ratio of "good" and "bad" robots.
